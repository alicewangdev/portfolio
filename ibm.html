IBM Internship


Working in the fields of Design and Engineering

Role UX/UI Designer, Lead Visual Designer, Software Solutions Engineer

Duration May 2020 - May 2022




I've had the opportunity of working at IBM in both Design and Engineering!

I was first hired as a full time intern, working for 16 months from May 2020 to August 2021.

Then, I was offered to return as a part-time intern for another 8 months from September 2021 to April 2022 while finishing up my final year of university.



UX/UI Design ✏️

Working with the IBM Sterling Order Management System team!

IBM Sterling Order Management System (OMS) is a software that tracks and manages the lifecycle of an order, used globally by high scale corporations. After an order is placed by a customer, the software helps the business coordinate the fulfillment process such as order entry, order sourcing, inventory management, and after sales services. It gives businesses live insights into its inventory and processings.

I worked in this team as a UX/UI Designer to build an internal documentation website

In this role, I explored the current troubleshooting process for IBM Support Engineers and how that can be enhanced using a digital solution.

I designed and implemented a database-driven internal website that stores and organizes important team documents, run books, and protocols. The website is now live and is accessed daily by IBM Sterling's Global Support Engineering team.

This project exercised my ability to create concept sketches, user flows, usability studies, and low and high-fidelity mockups.

With the website implemented and live and in use, IBM saw a 90% increase in troubleshooting efficiency and employee productivity. 🏆



Visual Design 👩‍🎨

Working with the IBM Radio team!

IBM Radio is an internal radio channel that is listened to by thousands of IBMers all over the world, it features several popular segments such as Intern Radio Hour.

I led a team of 2 graphic designers and 2 video editors to produce on-brand promotional graphics and videos for Intern Radio Hour, we used IBM's design thinking methodologies to brainstorm new concepts for internal social media campaigns which ultimately increased weekly listeners by 120%.

In this role, I worked with design software such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Final Cut Pro X, along with organizational softwares such as Trello.

The promotional contents created were very well received by IBMers and IBM management, being consistently re-used for major IBM events to promote the radio show. 🏆



Software Solutions Engineering 💻

Working with the IBM Sterling Order Management System team!

This role was a client-facing engineering role that allowed me to work with IBM clients which consists of trillion dollar software companies, multinational retailers, and additional high profile corporations.

In this role, I resolved deep technical issues related to the Sterling Order Management System software across SaaS and On-Premise cloud infrastructure by applying knowledge of User Interface Design, Software Architecture, Docker, Kubernetes, SQL, XML, and Java.

By actively identifying and assessing clients' needs through effective diagnosis, troubleshooting, and resolution, I was able to achieve a year-to-date NPS of 100%, one of the highest in our global team. This was formally recognized by IBM's Director of Global Client Success. 🏆
